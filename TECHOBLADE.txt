TECHOBLADE

Technoblade, whose real name was Alexander, was a famous American Minecraft YouTuber known for his skill, humor, and competitive gameplay. He started his YouTube journey in 2013, mainly playing Minecraft on the Hypixel server. Over time, his talent in PvP (player vs player) battles made him one of the most respected players in the Minecraft community.

He became widely popular in 2019 after winning multiple Minecraft Monday tournaments and later joined the Dream SMP in 2020, where his content reached millions more viewers. Technoblade also had a friendly rivalry with Dream, and he famously won a $100,000 duel hosted by MrBeast.

In 2021, Technoblade revealed he was suffering from cancer. Despite treatment, he sadly passed away in June 2022 due to metastatic sarcoma. At the time of his death, he had around 11 million subscribers, and his channel continued to grow after his passing, reaching 20 million subscribers by 2025.
